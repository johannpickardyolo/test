Visibility

We provide full employee visibility on an individual level by role occupied. Reports can be automatically generated and sent off to relevant stakeholders or dashboards can be used intuivitly explore data. Performance reports over extended timelines can be automated to show individual. 

Objective feedback

By measuring employee response to time triggered and event driven tasks, individual performance is measurable over time. Objective feedback and individual education models can be given objectively enabling career progression. 

Reliable performance

By allocating tasks dynamically, according to role and employee status in real time - measurable, accountability is encouraged, tasks get done in a reliable way with little to no manager intervention or follow up action.
